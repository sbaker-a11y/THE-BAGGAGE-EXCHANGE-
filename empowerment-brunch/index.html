Empowerment Brunch

A seat at the table.

The Empowerment Brunch is the easiest lane to merge in. A gathering over good food where women meet, exhale, and take the first step of the H.O.P.E. journey together. No pressure and no heavy lifting on this day. Just an hour and a half to two hours of real connection, a warm welcome, and a first taste of what it feels like to set something down.

Request a brunch See what is included

What to expect

An easy, open door.

Come as you are. The brunch is built to feel light, warm, and welcoming from the first hello.

THE SETTING

A warm table

Good food, good company, and a safe room to begin.

THE WORK

A gentle first step

An easy first taste of the H.O.P.E. journey, no deep diving required.

THE WARM FRONT DOOR

Where women trade what weighs them down for what lifts them up. A warm community walking the H.O.P.E. journey together, through Healing, Opportunity, Purpose, and Empowerment. Rooted in the Carolinas and in Black women, it welcomes women everywhere to set something heavy down and carry something lighter forward.
