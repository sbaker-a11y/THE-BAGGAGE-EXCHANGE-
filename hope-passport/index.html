EXPERIENCES · THE PASSPORT

The H.O.P.E. Passport

Stamp the journey. Win along the way.

Your H.O.P.E. Passport is the record of your journey and your reason to keep going. Collect a stamp at every experience you attend. Every stamp tells your story, and every in-person stamp enters you into drawings for prizes that help you move forward.

Start your PassportFind an experience

What it is

More than a keepsake.

The Passport is the through-line across every lane. You carry it from the Brunch to a Workshop to a Retreat and beyond, and it fills with stamps as you go. One story of how far you have come.

SIX EXCHANGES · SIX STAMPS · ONE FOR EVERY LANE YOU TRAVEL

More than a story

Every stamp is an entry.

Here is what makes the Passport different. Every in-person stamp you collect enters you into drawings for real prizes, chosen to help you keep moving forward. The more you show up, the more stamps you earn, and the more chances you have to win.

EVERY STAMP COUNTS

An entry each time.

Each in-person stamp enters you into the drawings automatically. No extra step to take.

MORE STAMPS, MORE CHANCES

Keep showing up.

The more experiences you travel, the more stamps you earn, and the better your odds.

PRIZES TO COME

Worth showing up for.

We are building a rewards lineup now. Watch this space for what you can win.

Two ways to collect

However you show up, you are in.

Everyone earns stamps. Here is how each kind counts.

IN-PERSON

Show up, get stamped.

Attend an experience and earn a stamp in your Passport. In-person stamps are what count toward the prize drawings. Every stamp is an entry, so the more you attend, the more chances you have.

LIVE PODCAST

Tune in live.

Listening from home? Download a digital H.O.P.E. Passport and earn a digital stamp every time you join a live podcast. New live episodes happen monthly. Attend enough of them and you are entered into a raffle of your own.

You earn a stamp whatever your entry point. For the prize drawings, only stamps earned at in-person experiences count. Live podcast listeners collect digital stamps toward a separate raffle for joining live shows.

How it works

Four simple steps.

Show up, collect, and let the stamps do the rest.

01

02

03

Each in-person stamp enters you into the prize drawings automatically.

04

Win along the way

Drawings happen through the year. The more you show up, the more chances you have.
