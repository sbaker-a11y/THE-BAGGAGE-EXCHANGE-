About the show

A lighter space for everything you carry.

THE Baggage Exchange Podcast is a lighter space for everything you carry. Dr. Shay sits with the trauma, the wins, and the weird in between, sometimes solo and sometimes with guests, and trades some of it for something lighter, moving through Healing, Opportunity, Purpose, and Empowerment. Real stories, lighter bags, about a half hour at a time.

How every episode moves

The four H.O.P.E. beats.

Each episode walks the same four beats, the same journey the whole brand runs on, in about thirty minutes.

BEAT 01

Bag Check

Name what you are carrying. The honest weight, said out loud.

BEAT 02

Plot Twist

The turn in the story, where things changed or could have.

BEAT 03

The Spark

The opportunity or insight hiding inside the hard part.

BEAT 04

One Bold Move

The single step forward you can take from here.

What to expect

Short, honest, and easy to carry.

No long lectures and no fixing. Just real conversation that helps you set something down and pick up something lighter, wherever you are listening from.

✦

About thirty minutes

an episode made to fit a commute or a walk.

✦

Solo and guests

Dr. Shay on her own, or with guests who get it.

✦

No fixing

real talk that lightens the load instead of adding to it.
