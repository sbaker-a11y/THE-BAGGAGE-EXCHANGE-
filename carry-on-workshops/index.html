Carry On Workshop

Get unstuck. Carry on.

The Carry On Workshop is for women who feel stuck. Stuck on the next move in your career, your relationship, your degree, your whole life. In one focused day you learn the H.O.P.E. blueprint and work it in real time, with Dr. Shay guiding you and a small group of women doing the same brave thing. Think vision board, but on steroids. You unpack the weight that has your brain locked and your feet planted, and you walk out with a plan you can actually move on.

Request a workshopSee what is included

What it is

A vision board on steroids.

This is not a lecture and it is not a pep talk. It is a working day. You learn the blueprint that carries a person from a heavy bag to a lighter life, then you work it on your own stuck place, live, with Dr. Shay in the room. Learning and doing, at the same time.

THE BLUEPRINT

Learn the blueprint

The H.O.P.E. Curriculum, taught live, so you leave knowing the method, not just the mood.

GROUP COACHING

Coached in real time

Dr. Shay guides the room while you work your own next move, alongside women who get it.

THE WORK

Unpack what is stuck

Name the weight that has your brain locked and your feet planted, and decide what stays and what goes.

YOU LEAVE WITH

A plan you can move on

Not a feeling. A plan. Clear next steps you can act on the very next day.

Who it is for

For the woman who is ready to move.

You already know something has to change. A career you have outgrown, a relationship that keeps you small, a degree you keep circling, a life that feels stalled. You are not broken and you are not lost. You are just carrying too much to move. This is where you set it down and find your footing again.

Where it fits

The brunch names it. The workshop unpacks it.

If you have been to an Empowerment Brunch, this is your next step, a full day to go deeper with a room that already speaks the language. If you are starting here, welcome. You do not need the brunch first.

HER Retreats

The biggest, heaviest bag, wear and tear and all. Days away for the deepest work.

It is right there in the name. You sort your carry-on, the weight you have been hauling, so you can carry on. Keep moving. Pick your journey back up. You come in stuck, and you leave ready to carry on your journey.

Coaching, not therapy. The workshop is guided group coaching built on the H.O.P.E. Journey. It is growth work, not clinical care. If you are in crisis, we will help you find the right support.

You leave with

A working H.O.P.E. Bag.

Every Carry On Workshop sends you home with a working H.O.P.E. Bag, so the practice keeps going long after the session ends.
