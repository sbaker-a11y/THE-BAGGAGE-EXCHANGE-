Carry On Workshop

Practice the work.

The Carry On Workshop is where the work gets hands on. In a focused session you practice real skills, boundaries, reframing, grounding, the tools that move you along the H.O.P.E. journey. You leave with something you can actually use, plus a working bag to keep practicing at home.

Request a workshopSee what is included

What to expect

Roll up your sleeves.

Less talking about the work and more doing it. You leave with skills in hand, not just ideas.

THE FORMAT

A focused session

A few hours of guided, hands on practice.

THE WORK

Real skills

Boundaries, reframing, and grounding, practiced not just discussed.

WHO IT IS FOR

Ready to dig in

For women who want to move past the first step.

YOU LEAVE WITH

A working bag

A session workbook and a tool tied to the skill you practiced.

You leave with

A working H.O.P.E. Bag.

Every Carry On Workshop sends you home with a working H.O.P.E. Bag, so the practice keeps going long after the session ends.

PRACTICE THE WORK

Where women trade what weighs them down for what lifts them up. A warm community walking the H.O.P.E. journey together, through Healing, Opportunity, Purpose, and Empowerment. Rooted in the Carolinas and in Black women, it welcomes women everywhere to set something heavy down and carry something lighter forward.
