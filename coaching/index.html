EXPERIENCES · COACHING · CCH

The exchange, one-on-one.

Private coaching with Dr. Shay and our team.

Some bags are easier to open with someone beside you. Coaching is the private room where you do the work at your own pace, guided by someone who has carried real weight and found the way through. It is the H.O.P.E. System, applied to your life.

Start with a consultFirst, is this therapy?

Read this first

Coaching is not therapy.

Let me be clear, because it matters. Dr. Shay is a licensed clinician, and that work lives at her clinical practice, not here. Coaching through THE Baggage Exchange is a separate, non-clinical service. It is not therapy, counseling, diagnosis, or treatment, and it does not create a clinician-patient relationship. Coaching supports your growth. It does not replace mental health care.

WHAT COACHING IS

Forward motion

Coaching looks ahead. We focus on goals, growth, and action. We name what you are carrying, decide what to set down, and build the next chapter on purpose. You leave with clarity, a plan, and someone holding you accountable to it.

WHAT COACHING IS NOT

Not clinical care

Coaching does not diagnose or treat mental health conditions. It is not therapy, crisis support, or a substitute for care from a licensed provider. If clinical help is what you need, that is a different and important door. Dr. Shay and her team will help you find it.

If you are in crisis or thinking about harming yourself, please reach out to a licensed professional, or call or text 988 in the United States to reach the Suicide and Crisis Lifeline. Coaching is built for growth, not for emergencies.

How it works

The method, made personal.

Same exchange you see across the brand, slowed down and built around you. We move at your pace, one bag at a time.

1

We start with the bag

You name what you are carrying. No performance, no fixing yet. Just an honest look at the weight.

2

We take honest inventory

We sort it into three piles: what is purposeful, what is questionable, and what serves no purpose at all.

3

We repack with purpose

What serves you comes with you. The rest gets set down for good. It does not go back in the bag, not even just in case.

4

We build forward

Healing, Opportunity, Purpose, Empowerment, applied to your real life, with steps you can actually take between sessions.
