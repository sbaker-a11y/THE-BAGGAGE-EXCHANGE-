EXPERIENCE LANE · HER 03

HER Retreats

Go all the way in.

HER Retreats by THE Baggage Exchange are the deep-dive experience for women ready for immersive restoration. You travel for three to four immersive days within the United States or five to seven abroad, walking the full H.O.P.E. journey across a held, trauma-informed space. Guests set the heaviest things down, reframe the whole story, connect their lived experience to a calling, and leave with tools, a plan, and the confidence to step forward. This is where you dive deep into the H.O.P.E. curriculum and move through Healing, Empowerment, and Restoration. You leave with the fullest H.O.P.E. Bag of any experience.

HER 03

What HER stands for

Healing. Empowerment. Restoration.

HER names the three movements of the deepest work. Over a few immersive days, you walk all three, all the way through.

H

Healing

Make space to name what you carry, feel safe, and set the heaviest bags down.

E

Empowerment

Find your voice, your clarity, and your next bold move, with the group beside you.

R

Restoration

Rest, restore, and put yourself back together lighter than you arrived.

The experience

A few immersive days, all the way in.

Small, intentional, and unhurried. You step away from the noise and give the work real time.

DURATION

3 to 7 days

Three to four days within the United States, or five to seven abroad.

SETTING

Safe and restorative

A calm, private setting chosen to let you exhale and go deep.

THE WORK

Full H.O.P.E. curriculum

The complete journey, guided, from Healing through Restoration.

THE PACE

Deep and unhurried

Room to feel it, name it, and set it down without rushing.

You leave with

The fullest H.O.P.E. Bag of any experience.

Every in person exchange sends you home with a H.O.P.E. Bag. HER Retreats send you home with the fullest one, so the work keeps going long after you land.

  • A complete journal and the H.O.P.E. Journey workbook.
  • A curated set of affirmation and ritual cards.
  • A signature keepsake to wear or keep.
  • Resources to carry the work forward after you leave.
