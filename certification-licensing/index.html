The paths

Two ways to carry it.

Become certified to facilitate, or license the method for your organization. Either way, you are trained and supported.

FACILITATOR CERTIFICATION

Lead the work

Train to facilitate the H.O.P.E. Curriculum, with the practice and the credential to lead it well.

METHOD LICENSING

Run it in your rooms

License the method for your organization, with a written license and the right to deliver it.

MATERIALS AND TRAINING

Everything you need

Curriculum guides, session tools, and training so you deliver the work faithfully.

ONGOING SUPPORT

Never on your own

Continued guidance that keeps the work strong and true to its roots.

A note on permission

Granted in writing, protected on purpose.

Every certification and license is granted in writing by The Baggage Exchange LLC. The method, materials, and brand are protected so the work stays consistent and the community stays safe. That protection is what lets us trust you to carry it.

CARRY THE METHOD FORWARD

Where women trade what weighs them down for what lifts them up. A warm community walking the H.O.P.E. journey together, through Healing, Opportunity, Purpose, and Empowerment. Rooted in the Carolinas and in Black women, it welcomes women everywhere to set something heavy down and carry something lighter forward.
