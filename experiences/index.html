The H.O.P.E. Passport

Six exchanges, six stamps.

Every lane leaves its mark in its own ink. Attend an exchange, earn a stamp, and watch your passport fill until it tells the whole story of where you have been. Every in-person stamp also enters you into drawings for prizes that help you keep moving forward.

SIX LANES, ONE EXCHANGE

Where women trade what weighs them down for what lifts them up. A warm community walking the H.O.P.E. journey together, through Healing, Opportunity, Purpose, and Empowerment. Rooted in the Carolinas and in BIPOC women, it welcomes women everywhere to set something heavy down and carry something lighter forward.
