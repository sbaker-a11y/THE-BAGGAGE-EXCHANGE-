About the founder

Dr. Shay, in short and in full.

SHORT BIO

Two sentences

Dr. Shukairo Baker, DNP, known as Dr. Shay, is the founder of THE Baggage Exchange, a transformational lifestyle brand and podcast. A doctoral-prepared nurse practitioner, licensed clinical social worker, business owner, and trauma survivor, she turned her own journey of trading heavy baggage for a lighter life into the H.O.P.E. Journey, and she builds it from the Carolinas.

FULL BIO

The longer story

Dr. Shukairo Baker, DNP, is a Black woman, a doctoral-prepared nurse practitioner, a licensed clinical social worker, a business owner, and a trauma survivor. By 14 she had become a mother. By 19 she was a mother of two, one born prematurely with severe special needs. She kept moving. Through more loss and setback, she taught herself to trade her heaviest bags for lighter ones, and she named the path the H.O.P.E. Journey. She became a clinician many times over, earning her LCSW, LCAS, APRN, PMHNP, FNP, and CNE. She went from weighing more than 350 pounds to skydiving at 15,000 feet over Portugal at 143, proof in her own body that no baggage is too heavy to exchange. Today she leads THE Baggage Exchange from the Carolinas, teaching, speaking, and helping other women find their way through, one exchange at a time.
